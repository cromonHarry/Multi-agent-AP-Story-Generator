In the vibrant year of 2045, Kira Solari stands amidst her towering bioluminescent vertical farm, her hands embracing the rich, loamy soil that sustains her community. As tensions escalate with the arrival of the rival cooperative, Kira, Elian, and Rhea mobilize the community to host an immersive AR event during the harvest festival.
