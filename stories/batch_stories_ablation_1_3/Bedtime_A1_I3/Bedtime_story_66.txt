In the vibrant year of 2135, humanity has embraced the transformative power of dreams through advanced technology that blurs the lines between consciousness and reality. Citizens enter communal Dreamscapes, curated environments designed to heal and connect them through shared narratives. Elysia Wu, a dedicated Dream Architect, tirelessly works to create inclusive Dreamscapes, reflecting on her own insecurities as she yearns to bring diverse individuals together. Meanwhile, activist Aidan Rivers rallies his community for equitable access to these experiences, fighting against rising Sleep Equity Grievances. As societal tensions over Dream Access Rights begin to simmer, Dr. Noemi Chant scrutinizes the ethics behind corporate control over dream technologies, while artistic curator Rhea Kallian strives to foster unity through immersive art drawn from collective dreams. Together, their paths intertwine in a world where each sleep becomes a shared journey, yet the struggle for equality threatens to unravel their utopia.
