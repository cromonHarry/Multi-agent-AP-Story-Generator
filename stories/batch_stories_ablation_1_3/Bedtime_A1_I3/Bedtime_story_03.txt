In the year 2143, Aria Voss stands at the helm of a bustling Reverie Enclave, where the rhythmic hum of SomnoPods and whispering AI symbiotes fill the air, creating an atmosphere of shared anticipation. With sleepless nights haunted by societal inequities, she passionately rallies a crowd, urging the need for equitable access to AI-mediated sleep technology to combat the rising tide of 'Sleep Inequity.' Meanwhile, Kieran Mallory secretly programs the symbiotes, torn by the exploitation of his creations by Dream Cartels, while Selene Ignacio oversees the towering structure of privilege, wrestling with her choices. In this transformative world, Orin Zhang captures the vibrant narratives of the Dream Weaving Festivals, illustrating the profound connection between shared dreams and community healing, driving home the urgent need for societal change.
