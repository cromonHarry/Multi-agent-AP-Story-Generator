In the year 2147, society flourishes under the vibrant glow of bioluminescent organisms embedded in daily life, with communal gatherings celebrating Dental Wellness Festivals at the heart of this transformation. Liora Denton, a dedicated bioethicist, cultivates her living dental garden, advocating for equitable access to advanced dental technologies. Meanwhile, Sage Lumis unveils her latest immersive art installation, depicting the beauty of oral microbiomes, while Dr. Nova Voss browses through the latest AI-driven dental health analytics, preparing to enhance the community's connection to their evolving wellness narratives.
