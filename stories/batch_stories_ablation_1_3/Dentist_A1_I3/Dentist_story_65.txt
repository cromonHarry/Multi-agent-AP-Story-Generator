Outside, Ryn Morgan rallies members of the Smile Collectives, their radiant grins a beacon of hope for those denied dental enhancements. Meanwhile, Eli Voss attends a lavish gala, filled with the privileged, yet feels a creeping emptiness as he observes superficial connections. Nara Winston navigates the bustling atmosphere, journalistic instincts keen, ready to expose the stories beneath the radiant surface, showcasing both the utopia and the unseen fractures within this new society.
