In the year 2147, Elara Voss, the cunning CEO of BioSync, navigates the opulent corridors of her high-rise headquarters, where holograms of biometric data swirl like phantoms of lost identity. This fuels public outrage, igniting protests led by the collective, drawing more citizens into the battle for autonomy over their identities. Amidst the chaos, Dr. Reyes struggles with his role in a system where his innovations might enable further surveillance, leading him to contemplate a critical choice that could change everything.
