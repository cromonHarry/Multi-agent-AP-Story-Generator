In the year 2147, Homeworld, a sprawling tech metropolis, thrives under the rule of a powerful corporation known as EcoVerse. Recycling has transformed into an automated, high-tech amalgamation of AI systems and advanced molecular nanotechnology. Every citizen is required to wear a device called a C.R.A.F.T. (Comprehensive Recycling and Filtration Tool), which can analyze what individuals discard in real time and redirect it to the most efficient recycling options available. Amelia, an ambitious engineer working at EcoVerse, dreams of transcending her standard role and creating something that incorporates humanity into waste management. Ender, an idealistic street artist, thrives outside the corporate fringes, using found materials to create statements against over-consumption. Leo, Amelia’s loyal friend with cybernetic limbs, helps gather art supplies while harboring secrets that could shake their world. Sabine, EcoVerse's ambitious executive, prioritizes profit over ethics, temporarily tying the company to advancements threatening natural ecosystems.

The story unfolds as Amelia discovers troubling discrepancies within the EcoVerse recycling algorithms. While performing a routine maintenance check, she encounters a clutter of egregiously mismanaged biowaste headed for incineration. Concerned by the inability to address this oversight mentally challenged by her growing idea—a fully organic community embedment project, seeking partnerships with dissenters like Ender—she covertly reaches out to him through an inventive forged graffiti communication. Intrigued by her message, Ender meets with Amelia and embarks on a mission to expose the corruption of EcoVerse, unaware that Sabine is keeping a close watch on unusual activities within the corporate powerhouse.

Their respective quests intertwine dramatically when Leo, seeking to leverage his abilities as an underground tech orchestrator, accidentally pirouettes amidst EcoVerse’s core reporting facilities. Three sworn spies discover him, enmeshing him well within the unfolding conspiracy—green surfboards of non-compliance offering glimpses into mistrust aimed specifically at employees like Amelia. Sabine grows increasingly threatened as whispers of rebellion begin rattling the immense gilded machine she governs.
