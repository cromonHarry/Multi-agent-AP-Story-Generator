Enter Silas, a recycling neural engineer who has successfully transformed organic materials into energy. Complicating matters further, Malik, an old-school scientist, tirelessly endeavors to retrieve forgotten recycling techniques that he believes can work in tandem with the newer methods but finds himself marginalized in this fast-paced futuristic society.
