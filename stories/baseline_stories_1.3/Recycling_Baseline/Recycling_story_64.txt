In the year 2045, Earth has transformed into a hyper-integrated society where every facet of life is monitored and optimized through a sophisticated recycling system known as the Global Circular Network (GCN). The GCN governs the planet's resources by employing AIs capable of processing waste down to its atomic elements and efficiently redeploying them into new products, making waste practically obsolete. Communities are built around these circular economy hubs where people like Tasha, a passionate young engineer committed to improving the GCN, work diligently to fulfill corporate quotas. However, Tasha learns that deeper in the GCN’s operation lies a substantial disparity—Ian, her boss who oversees sector inspections, has nefarious ties to a rogue group exploiting the system. Tasha's world becomes a chaotic blend of assembly lines and sinister secrets as she dives into her investigation.
