At the heart of this story are four characters: Elara, a devoted Council environmentalist; Raj, an underground hacktivist challenging the system; Mia, an empathetic recycler who first discovered the properties of Recyclite; and Tarek, the physicist behind nanotechnology that creates devastating contradictions to Recyclite’s potential.
