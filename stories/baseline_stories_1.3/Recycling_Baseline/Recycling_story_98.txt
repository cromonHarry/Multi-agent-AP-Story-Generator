In the year 2145, Earth has transformed into a primarily arboreal sanctuary as nature reclaims urban landscapes through advanced green technology. However, the cornerstone of this rebirth is a revolutionary form of recycling known as Synthergy—an emergent blend of biopreservation and digital composting. Human ingenuity merged with artificial intelligence allows waste to be converted not merely into reusable materials but into functional lifeforms and energy sources. Citizens these days depend heavily on the renewable energy dispersed from Synthergy Towers, focusing their efforts on ethical living, minimal ecological footprint, and nurturing synthetic life instead of relying solely on the overstrained natural resources. This ecosystem brings forth four main characters: Lia, a type-A efficiency expert leading a team of tech recyclers; Felix, a curious eco-anthropologist studying the remnants of pre-Synthergy lifestyles; Niamh, a disillusioned Synthergy technician hiding a groundbreaking secret; and Aryan, a drone scavenger slighted against the socio-economic divides by those within green society.

As raw materials begin to dwindle, tensions rise within Elysium, the floating district blueprint where society's elite enjoy an untouched environment while the lower strata labor outside its lush landscapes. Lia’s team receives the ITS (Intensive Tumor Scrapping) upgrade, designed to enhance synthesis processes during high-waste festivals—occasions where organic refuse from upper districts bombards their recycling facilities. Felix, alongside Niamh, speeds toward grave consequences about exploring the ancient civilization patches scattered often misreported as decay, while Aryan plucks urban scraps from city-built voids, standing on its chandeliers reminiscent of old times rescuing thousand memories from the floors below.
