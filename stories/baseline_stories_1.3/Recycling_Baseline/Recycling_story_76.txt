In the year 2077, the world has transformed into a dazzling yet precarious place where advanced technology and overwhelming waste coexist. Cities are adorned with shimmering skyscrapers made from bio-engineered materials capable of self-repair, thanks to sophisticated recycling systems operated by the SynthCaps, autonomous machines designed to rejuvenate old products and materials. At the heart of this rapid evolution lies a group of four unconventional friends: Luna, a passionate eco-activist; Rael, a tech-savvy inventor; Zara, a former corporate lawyer turned social entrepreneur; and Fitz, a resourceful scavenger with a keen eye for hidden treasures. United by a shared vision ignited by environmental degradation, they each relish in championing zero waste in their community, yet they are oblivious to the ideological rift beneath the glittering surface of this selective recycling world—a controversy between optimizing recycling for profit versus fighting against blending it into corporate greed.
