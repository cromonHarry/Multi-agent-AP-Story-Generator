This transformation has been spearheaded by Zane, a passionate environmental scientist who spearheads the Grevity Corporation, famous for developing nanotomic solvers — bots that disassemble waste materials down to atoms and reassemble them into new products. Accompanying him are Lira, an aggressive activist focused on increasing grassroots awareness; Conn, Zane’s stoic childhood friend and security chief at Grevity; and Mira, a brilliant engineer who constantly pushes the limits of the processors inside the solving machines. The tapestry of their interactions borders on complexity, primarily due to their diverging views on how to educate society about sustainability versus profit generation.
