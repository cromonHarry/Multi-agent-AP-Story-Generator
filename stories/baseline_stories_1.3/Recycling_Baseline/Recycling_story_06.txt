An advanced AI named CR-7, designed to maximize recycling efficiency, manages the complex ecosystem where people's waste feeds elaborate machines that re-process it into reusable networks of materials. At the center of this transformation are four pioneers: Leo, a passionate engineer striving for sustainability; Mina, a brilliant ecological psychologist studying the societal impacts of recycling; Astra, an artist harnessing reclaimed materials for her creations; and Noam, a gritty scavenger who once viewed waste as a source of personal gain but slowly sees the bigger picture. Within this clean-lined, vision-driven metropolis, under the glimmer of eco-friendly skyscrapers, the pinnacle of recycling flourishes, yet cracks appear beneath the hopeful surface.
