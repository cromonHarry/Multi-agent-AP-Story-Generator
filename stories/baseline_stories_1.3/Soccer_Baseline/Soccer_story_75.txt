In the near future, soccer has transformed into a technologically-enhanced sport known as Neosoccer, played in massive floating arenas high above megacities. Teams consist of both human players and advanced AI athletes, with games broadcasted in immersive AR experiences that envelop viewers in extraordinary virtual stadiums. Four main characters inhabit this thrilling world: Leo, a fiercely competitive human striker, genetically engineered for optimum performance; Ai Sol, a groundbreaking AI with advanced emotional algorithms and tactical prowess; Cassia, a skilled sports analyst who yearns to physically compete despite her disabilities; and Varrin, an underground rebel coach striving to reclaim the game from corporate control. The daring pursuit of authenticity leads each character to confront their ideologies and capabilities as the lines between human and machine blur.

As the globe gears up for the international Neosoccer championship, Leo's reputation is on the rise, and he aspires to be recognized solely for his talent, despite the frequent spotlight turned towards robotic players. Cassia, longing for empowerment, enters a local league aimed at integrating adaptive athletes into the sport, hoping to break societal stigma.
