In the year 2045, soccer has transformed into a hyper-technological spectacle known as Lumisphere Soccer, played in massive holographic arenas. Players don biologically augmenting suits that enhance their stamina, reflexes, and cognitive capabilities. Among them, we meet Jade Vela, a breakout star known for her dynamic plays and exceptional skills; Riko Lawson, her twin brother and a defensive strategist; Dr. Ellis Brahms, mad scientist turned coach who used to be a physicist before he embraced the ideals of this new sport; and Kaj August, a charismatic game-changer from the rival team, famed for his speed and extraordinary balance. Struggling against ignorance, Riko attempts to convince Jade to expose the illicit endeavors ruining what was once the “beautiful game.”
