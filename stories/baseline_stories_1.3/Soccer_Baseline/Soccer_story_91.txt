In the year 2045, the world has transformed dramatically, shaped by technology and societal shifts that have redefined the game of soccer. Mega cities dominate the landscape, and sporting arenas have evolved into dazzling environments enhanced with augmented reality. Traditional soccer, while still loved by many, has taken a back seat to the rise of Synergia, an immersive fusion of competitive soccer and virtual strategy. Among the pioneers of this rebel sport are Cora, a passionate former professional player turned tech-savvy coach; Axel, a charismatic star who’s never known life away from the field; Lina, a tactical genius renowned for her cerebral approach to both soccer and IT; and Marco, a fearless youth player aiming to prove his worth in both worlds. Together, they are the backbone of a revolutionary new team vying to dominate a league that challenges not just their physical skills but also their intellect and team synergy.

As the season progresses, the edge of rivalry sharpens. Cora devises an independently run Synergia Championship to highlight traditional values and infuse them with new strategies using evolving tech tools. As teams struggle between visually stunning exists and the core fundamentals of soccer, Lina believes running hundreds of simulations will give her team the unsurpassed advantage in play. However, momentum causes rifts among the players’ ideologies; younger talent like Marco desperately wants to push boundaries, embracing the future, whereas Axel clings to nostalgia for the purity of the original sport. The delicate balance among them begins to fracture, setting up both conflict and spirit against growing challenges—a rival team corruptly pushes boundaries by replacing human athletes with advanced AI, operated manipulatively to ensure unfaltering success on the league’s offensive.

With fears mounting and reputations on the edge, the championship approaches its climactic showdown between Cora's mixed-gender human team and the AI-assisted rivals, called Virt-Omax. Axel puts aside his reservations one last time and plays a decisive role in strategizing during the final match, driven by a desire to reclaim spontaneity in sports. The game itself showcases a kaleidoscope of technology and sheer human instinct. But mid-match, an unexpected glitch in the artificial player's behavior occurs, begging the question—what happens when innovative cherishment begins to obliterate spontaneity and instinctiveness, which is the essence of sports? Spirited debates rise, as each athlete battles inner doubt while finding controlled chaos in the rhythm of the game to reclaim the emotional heartbeat sorely missed.
