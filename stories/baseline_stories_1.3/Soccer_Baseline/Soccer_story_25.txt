In the year 2050, the world has reshaped its definition of soccer, integrating technology and unique gameplay that caters to a drastically altered sporting audience. In augmented reality (AR) stadiums, players don smart gear imbued with neurofeedback systems that augment their physical abilities and precision. Each match sees teams composed not only of human athletes but also AI-driven robots that can anticipate players' movements and enhance strategies. The story follows four key characters: Zara, a gifted teenage prodigy, whose precision has caught the attention of top teams; Dion, a seasoned captain struggling with the younger players’ ambitious aim to bypass him; Elara, visionary CEO of HyperSphere—a startup unveiling cutting-edge player suits; and Nico, an unseen rogue hacker who manipulates the system from afar, looking to disrupt the new technological era of soccer.

As soccer's popularity reaches unprecedented heights, the annual World Cup begins to draw attention worldwide. Zara and her team, the Dreamwalkers, are uncovered as the anticipated underdogs, while Dion's Elite Squadron holds the title for defending champions.
