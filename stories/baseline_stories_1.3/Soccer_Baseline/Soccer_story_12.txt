In the neon-drenched megacity of Neo-Futuria, soccer has transformed from a mere sport into a complex blend of cutting-edge technology and human athleticism known as "Hyper Soccer." The game is played within enormous stadiums equipped with advanced holographics, designed to revive matches in life-like 3D visuals. Talented players wear sensory gloves and bio-fit suits, amplifying their skills with motion-enhancing exoskeletons and brain-computer interfaces that allow them to process game situations within milliseconds. At the heart of this transformed experience are four aspiring stars: Laia, a drone engineer turned player known for her strategic brilliance; Nyas, the brash goal-scorer whose charismatic approach captures the fascination of fans; Miko, an astro-athlete with undying devotion to the sport, and Reya, the game's spoiled soul trending for being more about views than victory. Little do they know, intrigue lurks behind the glimmering surface of this athletic revolution.

As preparations ramp up for the International Hyper Soccer Championship, emerging fractures surface in the team's dynamics. Facing intense pressure, Nyas struggles with crippling past failures that resurface due to the public's insatiable need for dramas, while Laia grapples with greed from the corporate sponsors who intend on weaponizing player technology for market manipulation. Reya's antics grow more erratic on social media, attracting substantial attention but jeopardizing what they originally cared about most—soccer. Miko seeks to unite them beyond alliances molded by fame and infamy and pushes for cohesive teamwork, hoping to unveil their true potential on the field. Yet within weeks of competition, whispers of an underhanded scandal involving an idea for implanting technology directly into the players’ brains unfurl deep threads of suspense.

As the championship unfolds, Laia uncovers a dauntless plan: a faction of rogue engineers aims to mal-function the game by signing them up for avant-garde modifications—a method to control results and anchor them to audience satisfaction. Testing her available resources ultimately leads to a desperate showdown during the semifinals against DRX, a ruthless rival team aware of the modifications before it dawned on Laia’s squad. During the pivotal game, the tensions reach a fever pitch, each player forced to question both their ethical standings and their commitment to one another, igniting latent resentments and pushing desperately to even compete with mind-molding tech against the undercurrents.

Clown used ceaselessly for their advantage, tensions explode as the players, couched in a mesmerizing showdown on screen, discover contradictions to personal obsessions of the cyber-enhancements.
