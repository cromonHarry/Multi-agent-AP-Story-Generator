That transformation is attributed to advanced technologies, rule changes, and societal shifts ingrained by climate anxieties and resource scarcity. The centerpiece of this narrative follows Mira, a tenacious adaptive athlete who struggles with her identity as a human player in a world that leans on tech over talent. Her best friend and tech wizard, Zane, accompanies her on this journey, conflicted by tournament career ambitions and burgeoning ethical dilemmas surrounding augmented abilities. Join them are Playmaker, an AI bot designed to help strategize during matches, and Alexi, Mira’s mentor, who embodies legacy and authentic human institution as he reminisces about the glorious days of pure, unaltered play.
