In the year 2045, the world has entered a new age of synthetic food production, driven by climate instability and food scarcity. The conventional coffee bean is a rarity, largely replaced by an advanced bioengineered organism known as the Nex-Bean, which can yield pure coffee essence and adapt to different flavor profiles on demand. This new process is managed by robotic crop tenders, controlled by a conglomerate named BrewCorp, that produces coffee at unprecedented rates and controls the global coffee market. Four individuals navigate this transformed reality: Aiden, a barista with a passion for traditional brewing; Mira, an environmental activist fighting against monopolistic control of coffee resources; Soren, a BrewCorp researcher adamant about the potential of Nex-Bean technology; and Ellie, a veteran explorer who remembers the taste of beans harvested from the highest peaks in Ethiopia. The cityscape is filled with Starbucks-style cafes powered by sophisticated machines, adding a veneer of familiar comforts over a plot entangled in environmental ethics and cultural loss.

As tensions rise, Mira rallies a community of coffee lovers to safe houses alongside Ellie, insisting that they pursue a reclaimed coffee experience over readily available synthetic brews. Together, they initiate a covert mission to discover the last remaining coffee plantations hidden in the remote mountains. The team faces consumer apathy, corporate surveillance drones, and climate challenges, all while cultivating a network of support among other activists. Meanwhile, Aiden grows increasingly conflicted between his yearning for authenticity and the security offered by BrewCorp's synthetic options, complicating his loyalty as he witnesses even his friends discard traditions for easy access to caffeine. Soren, while absorbed in perfecting the Nex-Bean process, starts to suspect invading mind-hued euphoria is responsible for altering collective priorities, creating tension as public eagerness shifts away from sustainability.

An unexpected series of communications lead Mira and Ellie to the revered nestled paradise atop the forgotten Mekwen Plateau. Upon arrival, they non-violently come into conflict with one of BrewCorp’s purification teams, claiming sacred authority to field, maintain, and expand living entities on behalf of new, responsible cultivation techniques.
