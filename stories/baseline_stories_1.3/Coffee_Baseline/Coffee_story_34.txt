In the year 2048, coffee has transcended from a mere beverage to an integral technology woven into the fabric of daily life. With global climate shifts making traditional coffee farming nearly impossible, Earth's largest coffee conglomerate, CafeX, reinvented the essential elixir. They introduce “Neuromatter,” a synthetic compound mimicking both the taste and the stimulating impulses of caffeine. This innovation has revolutionized the industry, eliminating agricultural dependencies and allowing anyone with the right tech to brew a customized experience using neural interface brewing rigs. Our main characters — Jasper, a barista-turned-codenamer/technology hacker; Kaylee, a climate activist whose family farms disrupted habitats for coffee; Victor, the profit-driven CEO of CafeX; and Healey, a passion-driven mixologist who believes coffee is an art form — stand at the intersection of tradition and technological evolution.

As society simmers in neuromantic cafes and virtual drink journalism, an underground group called the Brews, led by Kaylee, begins to uncover the darker side of Neuromatter. They find that the substance inhibits creativity and inhibits bodily energy in the long run despite its initial stimulating rush.
