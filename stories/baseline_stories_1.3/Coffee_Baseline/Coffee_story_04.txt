In the year 2045, humanity's reliance on coffee had evolved into a highly sophisticated pastime rather than a mere caffeinated indulgence. Bioengineered coffee beans, known as AstroBeans, were cultivated in vertical hydroponic farms on floating platforms in the sky, imbuing them with adaptogenic properties—a blend of nutrients scientifically crafted to enhance resilience, focus, and mood regulation. Eloise, a passionate barista and 'Flavor Alchemist', had recently started experimenting with AstroBean extract, hoping to unlock its maximum potential. Teddy, her skeptical ex-husband and journalist, aimed to expose the industry's hidden impacts, while Mai, Eloise's ambitious programmer friend working at Zenith Corp, held beta access to a groundbreaking virtual coffee-simulating technology. Lastly, there was Rufus, a whimsical coffee flare artist, who managed to incorporate his profound artistic smokiness into futuristic latte displays, causing their café—Flavor Lab—to be a front in the coffee exploration renaissance.

Eloise believed that she could take her flavor simulations offering to new heights using the advanced pouring techniques of Rufus combined with Mai’s new tech. They planned to revolutionize the coffee experience by allowing patrons to immerse themselves in a multi-sensory environment that would tailor coffee brews according to emotions they input through compact neural headsets. This attracted the attention of Zenith Corp’s CEO who was fascinated with making a server model coffee machine that customized brews perfectly suited to a patron’s requests. As the trio began crafting their prototype over weeks, Teddy gathered evidence revealing Zenith had been neglecting serious health risks linked to synthetic dependencies resulting from AstroBeans, but they failed to recognize the mounting pressure building on them as the company scheduled a conveyance of the testing Café as a full-blown experiment target.

One fateful wrap-up event came closer each day, and it was when they officially unveiled their prototype: "Emold," an AI-integrated brewing system tasked explicitly with altering emotional parameters in a safe manner using sensory elements.
