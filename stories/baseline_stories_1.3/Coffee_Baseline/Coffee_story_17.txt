In the not-so-distant future, as urban societies immerse themselves in fast-paced technology, traditional coffee has undergone a profound transformation. Improved bioengineering techniques and proprietary harvesting methods have birthed highly specialized coffee beans, each designed to uniquely cater to different neurobiological needs. For investors and industrial shadowy giants, “Dopacoffee,” a neural-enhancing brew, reigns supreme. Set in the vibrant, neon-lit streets of Neoterica City, the opening days of the global launch with ULink Enterprises cements its dominance, touting a product that promises customized energy levels, focus enhancement, and even a boost in empathy. But four unlikely friends – sustainability advocate Mira, rebellious barista Leo, neuro-hacker Andre, and fearless journalist Elara – apprehend the dangers concealed behind a cup nearly laced with corporate goodwill.

Tension arises when Leo, suspicious of ULink's high-handed methods of cultivation and aggression against independent coffee growers, uncovers alarming regulations in encrypted corporate documents. Inspired by his revelations, Mira strives to expose ULink’s monopoly over their anonymous harvesting of nutrient-depleted coffee. As they dive deeper into ULink’s operations, joining forces with ace hacker Andre, this banter in this trendiest coffee shop starkly develops into a grassroots movement. Meanwhile, fiercely dedicated journalist Elara seeks integrity and click-worthy reports to fight the alienation that artificial coffees have brought to cult caffeine culture. Discovery of scheming trade agreements leads them toward perilous entanglements where corporate espionage intertwines with personal passion.

The stakes climaxes when ULink, realizing that public speculation could lead to audits and revelations, deploys an authority-laden ambush during Mira's and Leo’s campaign rally. They point out the pulse-stimulation effects that DopaCoffee might have on consumers, exacerbating society’s tech dependency.
