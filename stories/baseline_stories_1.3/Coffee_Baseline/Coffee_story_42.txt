In the year 2042, coffee has been revolutionized by both advancements in biotechnology and the spiraling crises in climate change that have almost entirely eradicated traditional coffee plants. Geneticists have created a synthetic coffee substitute called "CafeSynthe," a liquid brewed from algae designed to mimic the taste and effects of traditional coffee through bioengineering. The protagonist, Elara, is a passionate barista at one of the remaining premium coffee shops in Neo-Eden, a bustling metropolis situated where the Amazon Rainforest used to thrive. At her side is Kael, her childhood friend, now a climate scientist on the front lines of the battle against climate change technologies. Together with Ava, an unapologetic black-market trader, who sources original coffee beans from protected patches of wilderness, and Zane, a coffee-connoisseur with a penchant for the refined flavors of traditional coffee, they intertwine their lives in one cup at a time, attempting to rejuvenate the lost taste.

As Neo-Eden celebrates its Annual Coffee Festival entitled "Revival," excitement mounts due to a legendary subplot that a seedling of true coffee has been uncovered—a rumor starting to sweep the underground circles championed by Ava. Her last untouched stash of beans has drawn the attention of Antiphon, an authoritarian conglomerate attempting to monopolize CafeSynthe and eradicate original coffee.
