In the year 2045, coffee had transformed from a simple plant-based beverage into a complex blend of nutrients and bioengineered ingredients, cherished for both its energizing effects and augmented capabilities. Ground primarily in vertically-integrated hydroponic farms amidst sprawling megacities, coffee had become a part of daily life, consumed not just for taste but as a prerequisite for productivity in a world dominated by competition and constant connections. Among those drawn into this world were Mara, an ambitious barista-scientist mixing her passion for flavors with a cutting-edge molecular system; Janek, an ambitious corporate industrialist seeking a monopoly in coffee culture; Lydia, an old-fashioned enthusiast clinging to rustic traditions in a glittering high-tech world; and Ox, an AI entity programmed to optimize the flavors and benefits of coffee for the masses. Each lived according to their relationship with coffee: artisanal craftsmanship, raw ambition, heritage, and calculated precision.

As the world's addiction to “TurboExtract,” a rapid coffee variant infused with energizing compounds and neural enhancers, swelled, environmental concerns emerged. The once preferred coffee beans were becoming depleted, prompting a duel between advanced biogenetic coffee alternatives mimicking genuine flavors and natural “classic brews” celebrated for their purity. Mara, excited by the prospect of developing sustainable yet complex flavors to add to the TurboExtract lineup, sought support from the powerful Janek. In contrast, Lydia setup her “coffee way” community, providing mentored workshops emphasizing organic cultivation methods. Tensions grow between pod segments: supporters of biogenetics battles old samplers loyal to authenticity, bewildered on which version to embrace.

Data on social media shifted drastically with every release of a new TurboExtract concoction while immersive "sip-trip" experiences skyrocketing in popularity enabled virtual caffi-focus calming portals to distract caffeine-addicted working spectators.
