These remarkable organisms are capable of producing high-caffeinated, nutrient-rich droplets that can be collected and consumed. The protagonist, Mira, a fervent anti-technology barista, runs a quaint coffee boutique called 'Signals.' Meanwhile, Augustus, a corporate mogul of Crème la Crème’s A.I.-powered Coffee Extraction, targets Mira’s unconventional shop, aiming to replace her with an automated coffee pod dispensing synthesizer. His child, Theo, seeks another way, longing for the authentic, rich taste of traditional coffee, questioning the oversight of human connection in a machine-driven age. Hela, a barista who once worked at Crème la Crème, harbors insider knowledge about fundamental flaws in Augustus's technology while battling her own dilemma of loyalty to a company that provided her a starting platform.
