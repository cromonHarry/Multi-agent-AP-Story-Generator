In the year 2045, the world has shifted drastically due to the influence of biotechnology and climate change. Properties of traditional coffee beans have been combined with synthesized compounds, giving rise to "Neurobrew," a beverage manufactured to not only enhance energy but also to directly tap into the brain’s neurochemistry for heightened focus, creativity, and mood enhancement. Harvesting now endows farmers with modified crops resistant to climate fluctuations, making coffee production more sustainable yet sparking protests among traditionalists. Amidst this storm, Ava, a determined agricultural scientist nurturing her family’s Romaridge coffee plantation, dreams of restoring original coffee varieties. Graham, her skeptical best friend and a digital resale entrepreneur, is caught up in the brewing corporate mergers, while Rhea, a negligent Metro City councilwoman driven by flashy tech trends, passionately champions Neurobrew branding, promoting it as a socio-economic revolution. Last but not least, Felix, a former barista turned traveler who longs for genuine connections, moves between cities searching for merely a taste of the authentic brew.

As Neurobrew gains massive popularity and media exposure, Ava becomes skeptical of Rhea's glossing over the beverage’s negative effects. Strained by time amid personal setbacks and incited by unembellished community feedback, she creates a cloning experiment aiming to grow the world’s rarest knot of Arabica coffee variety in an alternative medium on her family’s sunflower-farmed land. Although she brings unity to her confused peers, including Felix - unsure how to balance heritage preservation with inspiring youthful minds in her café pop-ups - Graham’s corporation pairs with Rhea to usurp Ave and lead a PR crest promoting an ill-considered ‘Unleashing Neurobrew’s Superpower Campaign’ nationwide. Feeling defeated, the ragtag group realizes individuality can’t end with fried concepts as opposition from simplistic neophiliac consumers becomes emotionally wrought— rappelling destruction raced alienation leave their café buzzing with hushed disappointment.
