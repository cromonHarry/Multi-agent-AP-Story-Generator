In the year 2045, society has transformed as humanity struggles with depleted natural resources and advancing technology. Coffee, once brewed, transformed into various bioengineered forms during the Energy Crisis of 2023. Chloroplast-infused hydro grounds flourish in vertical farms—where vegetables and plants once stood—transforming the beverage into a powerful source of oxygen and sustenance. She works alongside Klaus, her cynical but brilliant chemist partner, whose developments in molecular flavor enhancement play a crucial role in achieving their goal. Meanwhile, they are pursued by Bertha, the ambitious CEO of an influential coffee conglomerate that threatens to monopolize this bio-revolution. Approximately half as determined is Stylz, a once-popular virtual reality coffee artist who taps into designer habitats where he provides calming atmospheres powered by VR simulations.
