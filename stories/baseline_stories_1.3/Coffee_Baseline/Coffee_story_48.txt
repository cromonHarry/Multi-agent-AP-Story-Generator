In the year 2045, the world has changed beyond recognition. Climatic upheavals and advancements in biotechnology have woven new threads in the fabric of daily life, and with them a transformed approach to one of humanity’s most cherished rituals: coffee consumption. Cella Rojas, a passionate barista and engineer, works at a Lumene Cafe, a high-tech establishment known for synthesizing café ambrosia, a nutrient-rich, multi-sensory coffee experience served via inhalable vapor. Meanwhile, Theo, a self-styled coffee purist who runs an underground café soon finds his traditional methods and dreams under siege. Together with Cella, they face a challenge that IKEA-Vọpụta Coffee Corp seeks to eradicate all artisanal coffee through a slick campaign touting the health benefits of synthesized caffeine. Max Kim, a fierce environmental activist intertwined in their cause, and Deirdre Beckett, a corporate espionage agent assigned to bring Lumene’s concept under corporate control, become unlikely allies, each with conflicting motivations.
