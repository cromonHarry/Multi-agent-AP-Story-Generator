In the not-so-distant future, the Earth has undergone significant ecological transformations, leading to an unusual melding of technology and traditional agriculture. Coffee, once a humble beverage, has evolved into a familiar staple in society, propagating a unique ecosystem known as the "Bean Zone." There, biotechnologists harness artificial intelligence to optimize growth conditions for the exceptional Sugarlute coffee plant, engineered for reduced water usage and empowered with self-nourishing abilities. Notaro, an eccentric coffee farmer determined to revive traditional farming methods; Ren, a brilliant AI specialist advocating for the cutting-edge technologies in agriculture; Lira, a food journalist on a quest to revive the romance of communion over shared caffeine, and Jax, a corporate barista wannabe with dreams of success against the Thunderhead corporation, complicate this idyllic yet rapidly-tailored future.

Conflict brews when a series of complications arise in the Bean Zone as the highly-controlled conditions cause genetic irregularities in Sugarlute beans, leading to adverse taste changes. Saccharinics, an artificially smart blend derived from Sugarlute, commands the coffee market, beloved for its sweetness but devoid of authenticity. As clients wane in their loyalty, and sales plummet, Notaro realizes that revolutionary farming practices might stabilize not only quality but community bonds containing rich legacies and heritage. Lira brings Notaro's plight to her followers, portraying the decline of 'raw' essence versus 'matrix' ingenuity, while Ren staunchly defends the merits of streamline technology that increased productivity to manage extreme soil degradation—the ideological clash radiating concern to many communities dependent on perpetual coffee sales.

During a high-profile coffee festival, a dramatic confrontation culminates. Tensions rise between agriculture advocates and academic technocrats over a newly opened exhibit from Thunderhead that boasts absolute replicability of taste with high yields.
