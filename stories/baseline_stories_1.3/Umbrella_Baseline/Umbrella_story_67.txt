In the year 2078, umbrellas have transcended their mundane beginnings. After centuries of evolution, the Umbreloid has taken center stage—a sleek, AI-driven device embedded with nanofeatures capable of sensing environmental changes. Marcos, an aspiring inventor, designs a new variant called Vision Umbrella, one that transcends mere weather protection. Vision Umbrella is linked to the users’ augmented reality implants, projecting a safe travel path and highlighting areas at risk of severe climatic events, effectively making it a personal environmental shield. Alongside Marcos is Fiona, a data analyst who is creating crucial predictive algorithms; Kurt, an underground journalist with a penchant for unearthing dark secrets; and Ike, a scientist still bruised from past failures tied to a company that mishandled ecological innovations. Together, they form an unlikely team focused on using technology to safeguard humanity in a world slowly deteriorating from climate change.

As the city transforms with increasingly erratic weather patterns, fissures appear in understanding this new society. During a development summit, Marcos showcases the prototype of Vision Umbrella. Interest spawns among investors, but a night of heavy rain brings chaos as the city buzzes with panic over losing everyday control of this anomaly called climate. Meanwhile, Kurt discovers an insidious truth: big corporations are planning to buy out the rights to the umbrella tech and siphon controlling data for profit instead of allowing distribution to the community. As tensions and desperation arise, Fiona overhears compromising conversations that reveal ideal environments created above these communities in inter-secure zones, protected from crude storms but living underneath extreme privilege technologically denying others the progress in weather innovation.

Compelled by environmental justice, the friends decide to infiltrate the corporate headquarters to gather evidence.
