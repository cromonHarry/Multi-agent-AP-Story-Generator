In the year 2045, climate change has resulted in extreme weather phenomena sweeping across cities worldwide. Amid this chaos, a biotech company has created a groundbreaking device known as the "Umbrella." This is no ordinary rain-protection tool; it's a nanobiomech suit that shields its user from all hazards including acid rain, toxic storms, and extreme temperature fluctuations. Taking the world by storm, Umbrella Tech has installed the Umbrella across metropolitan areas, but the suits are not without their controversies. Maya, a young, idealistic engineer working for Umbrella Tech, is excited about the possibilities; her skeptical friend Theo, a journalist, senses dark tales hidden underneath the surface. Meanwhile, an enigmatic security officer, Lexx, maintains order across the cities’ Umbrella grids, while Kazan, a brilliant hacker, operates in the shadows, committed to exposing the company’s deeper secrets.

As citizens increasingly depend on the Umbrella suits for their survival, undesirable effects begin emerging from their widespread use. Reports arrive at Theo about citizens experiencing unsettling side effects, from continuous encryption that affects cognition to emotional numbness due to prolonged interaction with the Umbrella’s environment. Utilizing the opportunity, Theo enlists Maya to assist in identifying the anomalies within Umbrella's technology. Together, with Lexx reluctantly drawn in as their rights and observations of society shift, they start to uncover evidence pointing toward a hidden agenda — a form of social control engineered by Umbrella Tech to suppress free will under the pretense of protection from the chaotic weather. Kazan provides encrypted intel as they strive to bring the real narrative to light: a dystopian threat disguised as salvation.

The trio discovers that Umbrella's ashes rose from the GREATER initiative, a governmental program designed both to combat and manage the disastrous climate events and to simplify subsistence strategies adjustable by controlling access to health-enhancing suits. When leaked footage reveals Umbrella drones scanning the city for dissent and harming anyone operating outside established parameters, tensions reach a boiling point. In an unprecedented strike, they launch a plan at Advantage Fair, a massive annual tech expo showcasing the Umbrella suits. Simultaneously enhancing collective unrest stoked particularly by disenfranchised sectors of residents feeling at risk for their lives they prompt protesting crowds joined from different lawns, urging the victory for empowerment.

As chaos unfolds with protests engulfing the expo descends into riot, individuals stuck on the enclosed Umbrella grid manifest the feeling that autonomy nearly drowned beneath synthetic tech.
