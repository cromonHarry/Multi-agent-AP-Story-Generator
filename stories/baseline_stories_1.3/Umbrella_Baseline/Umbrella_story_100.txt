In the year 2045, society has transformed significantly due to climate change, resulting in intense weather phenomena that make rainforests swallow cities and deserts bloom in erratic ways. This new ecosystem leads to the invention of Termispheres, cutting-edge domes that provide sustainable, climate-controlled environments for individual buildings and neighborhoods. Within these enclosed landscapes, bioengineered flora ensures fresh air and food production. At the heart of this evolution, the Umbrella Corporation, once notorious for environmental exploitation, pivots to creating advanced umbrella-tech: portable drones that can shield people from extreme weather by projecting customized climate control fields. Among those leading this charge is Sofia, a determined environmental scientist; Leo, a charismatic marketing executive with a mysterious past; Tessa, a wary journalist seeking the truth behind Umbrella’s transformation; and Orion, a disgraced former member of the corporation's board, notified about sinister activities he can only refer to as “Project Quietus.”

However, Leo begins to notice odd glitches during demonstrations — properties far too advanced for reported technology. Tessa reports in the news that major profit shifts seemed connected to concealed upgrades in drone tech authorized by Orion — dubiously branded as contracts for social good. While untrained in conspiracies, Sophia’s curiosity leads her to dig deeper into Umbrella's labs, suspecting they might mask darker ambitions behind their surface of ecological stewardship. Convinced that something unnatural is lurking beneath their shiny facade, each character becomes entwined in a web of ambition, ethics, and the lure of corporate greed.
