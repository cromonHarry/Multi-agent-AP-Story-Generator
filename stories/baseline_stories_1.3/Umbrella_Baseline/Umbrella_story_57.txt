In the year 2083, the world has transformed drastically due to rampant climate change and fractured political landscapes. Cities rise high into the sky, encapsulated in bio-domes that protect citizens from extreme weather and toxic atmospheres. At the heart of this survivalist venture is a cutting-edge biotechnology known as Umbrella, designed to shield populations not only from harsh environments but also to safeguard from the escalating dangers of spreading pandemics and societal conflicts. Each individual is fitted with a personal device, called Umbra-Tech, altering environmental readings into biological measures, thereby promoting personal safety at the expense of privacy. Four diverse individuals set the stage for the story: Kai, a curious tech-savvy teen experiencing the moral dilemmas of a surveillance state; Iris, a 65-year-old bioware engineer frustrated with unchecked technological ambition; Felix, a former activist seeking to dismantle the oppressive systems Umbrella has birthed; and Samara, a journalist desperate to expose the truth behind the shiny facades of engineered safety.

Esoteric murmurs about Umbrella’s true purpose grow louder daily as Kai discovers classified coding embedded within his Umbra-Tech. His attempt to decrypt the log reveals unsettling connections between Umbrella and a government security crisis operative named Elias, who seeks to use the technology as a mass control device disguised as a means of safety and welfare. Concerned about the implications, Kai seeks out Iris for knowledge. On their excursion, they hack a local facility with the unexpected help of Felix, who works underground as a flipped asset aiming to fortify disenfranchized futures. Their passionate yet risky alliance birth hope, ambition, and resistance; however, as they peel away layers of meticulously orchestrated identity shifts erected by Umbrella, Shadow Patrols begin to take notice.

The fear mounts as authorities turn a blind eye and begin tracking unauthorized gadgets. In a high-tension conference broadcasted live supposedly to applaud advancements in community safety, Felix takes center stage, abruptly presenting evidence of Umbrella's hidden agenda to present humanity as hapless subjects immune only as long as they submit into compliance. Paranoia escalates when shadowy figures descend upon the venue to seize complacency silencing the dissenters.
