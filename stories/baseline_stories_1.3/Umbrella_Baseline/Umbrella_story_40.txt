In the year 2042, humanity has become adept at harnessing advanced materials and biotechnology, spawning innovations in architectural design and environmental conservation. Sydney remains a sprawling metropolis known for its unpredictable climate—rainstorms one moment, blue skies the next. Central to this unpredictable weather is the Sentinels' Umbrella, a massive, AI-controlled perimeter that automatically expands and contracts based on a combination of weather forecasts and environmental data. Margot, a civil engineer passionate about sustainable design, has recently been promoted to oversee this massive infrastructure project. Paired with Ila, a young and ambitious scientist specializing in weather phenomena, the duo faces complex challenges in maintaining the Umbrella's efficiency while rebuilding privates and public trust after several malfunctions caused local unrest.

Rohan, a prominent journalist disillusioned by media cacophony, delves into the underpinnings of public opinion about the Umbrella. Margot and Ila believe Rohan’s investigative spirit could fuel a deeper understanding of rising destructive weather patterns. With ongoing urban maintenance expeditions and discussions about transparency, Margot agrees to collaborate with Rohan.
