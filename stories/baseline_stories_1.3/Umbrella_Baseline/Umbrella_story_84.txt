In the year 2045, climate change has reached critical levels, leading to frequent and unpredictable weather patterns. To combat extreme conditions, a global conglomerate known as UmbraCorp has engineered advanced personal shields - dubbed "Umbrellas." These are not just for rain but protect against lethal storms, radiation exposure, and harmful pollutants. Sophia, a brilliant young engineer working on UmbraCorp's cutting-edge project, discovers that the technology has also garnered military interest. Her close friend Leo, a passionate activist worrying about climate displacement, warns her about the potential misuse of the technology. Meanwhile, Vandor, a relentless military general, intends to use the Umbrellas for civil control rather than protection. The main characters find themselves wrapped in a web of conflicting purposes and ideals.
