In the year 2042, Earth had succumbed to climate madness. Temperatures hovered at extremes, recurring violent storms became a new normal, and secrets whispered through crowded cities. Major corporations monopolized disaster management technologies, branding urban metaville dwellers with subscriptions to weather management services. One such service offered “Umbrella,” a sentient weather shield accessible via holographic projection. As relentless thunderstorms fell upon their community, tension began to brew among the citizens. The Umbrella's ability to alter weather patterns often drew its users into crippling contracts. Those stationed above would flourishe, while families slipped into social upheaval.
