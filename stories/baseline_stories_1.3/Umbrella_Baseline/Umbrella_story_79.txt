Advanced nanotech had integrated with the environment, allowing weather modifications to enhance agricultural efficiency and streamline urban habitats. Amidst these implants, “Umbrella” evolved into a powerful, AI-driven company specializing not just in personal weather shields but, largely, in asteroid deflection services. The primary players included Elsie, a headstrong environmental engineer; Rian, a brilliant but aloof AI programmer; Zara, an ambitious news reporter, and Malik, the passionate CEO warning humanity about impending meteor impacts and the sacrifices of poor urban communities.
