In the year 2045, weather anomalies have become commonplace due to climate change escalated by years of political inaction. As storms rage uncontrollably across the globe, most cities have invested in innovative forms of weather protection. The symbol of this new age is the Umbrella Corporation, which has transformed ordinary umbrellas into smart weather shields. Capable of detecting shifts in weather patterns, these multi-functional devices wrap around individuals, creating a holographic protection shield that not only deflects the seeds from storm systems but also provides climate-managed environments. Among the inhabitants of Nova Vitae, a quickly-growing megacity built on the ruins of the old world, four individuals interface with the Umbrella systems in unexpected ways: Amelia, a brilliant young engineer who senses that their technology has hidden flaws; Jonas, her dogged investigative reporter boyfriend; Dr. Rachel Tan, the idealistic chief scientist at Umbrella Corp aiming for mass market innovation; and Sam, a hacker from the Underweb seeking to expose corporate secrets.

As the rainy season begins, an unusual weather pattern develops over Nova Vitae—a focused superstorm that destabilizes even Umbrella Corporation's vast range of protective measures. Amelia notices discrepancies in the data produced by her own device, hinting at possible sabotaging attempts by disgruntled Umbrella employees. After coming up short in answers from her research, Amelia confides in Jonas, who is well-versed in corporate machinery and its inner workings. Together, they begin to investigate discrepancies in public-friendly narratives pushed forward by Rachel and the umbrella tech failures experienced sporadically around them.
