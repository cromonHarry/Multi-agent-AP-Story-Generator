In the near future, Earth has become increasingly hostile due to climate change and rampant technological weaponization, catalyzing widespread migration toward megacities where survival relies heavily on innovation and adaptation. In this urban sprawl thrives the Imperium, a powerful corporation that produces "Umbrella," an advanced nano-fabric used primarily for personal protection against violent weather and augmented attacks. The protagonist, Asha, a resourceful street organic technician known for her innate ability to repair anything, accidentally stumbles upon a sabotage within the Umbrella production line during her clandestine infiltration for information. She is immediately thrown into a web of conspiracy involving her childhood friend Raj, an ambitious UI designer for the Imperium, and Mira, a disillusioned scientist who believes that Umbrella's potential benefits for humanity have been tainted. Tension escalates when Tyrik, a ruthless enforcer loyal to the Imperium, is sent to quell any dissent amid fears that Umbrella's recent enhancements might harbor a fatal flaw.
