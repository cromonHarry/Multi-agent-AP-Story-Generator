In the year 2045, the world has drastically changed due to unchecked climate change and severe weather conditions. Umbrellas, once simple devices designed to shield from rain, have evolved into multi-functional gadgets known as "Adaptive Shields." These high-tech devices, integrated with environmental sensors and AI technology, adjust their properties based on real-time weather forecasts and individual user preferences. They not only protect against rain but also provide shielding from UV radiation, save lives during downpours with rapid drainage systems, and even double as personal climate control units.
