In the year 2045, cooking technology has transformed dramatically thanks to advancements in nanotechnology and AI. At the center of a thriving suburban community, Orion Tews, a once-renowned chef turned stovetop designer, has innovated a flagship product called NeuraCook—a futuristic stovetop that learns from its user’s preferences. This device not only cooks but interacts seamlessly with an owner’s smart home system, anticipating needs via a central AI named Elysia that monitors everything from diet trends to environmental conditions.
