In the year 2057, cooking technology has metamorphosed dramatically due to the integration of artificial intelligence and synthetic food. The coveted invention of the Airtreat, an advanced "stovetop" that autonomously prepares any meal imaginable—sourcing ingredients from a holographic pantry directly infused into the machine—has taken the culinary world by storm. Our story follows Birch, a acclaimed chef who is grappling with the artistic loss of traditional cooking skills; Zephyr, a strong-willed food activist arguing for a return to organic, hands-on food; Nova, a witty AI chef subservient to Birch and programmed with centuries of culinary knowledge; and Rad, a grizzled engineer of cooking technology determined to transform the skyline metal city into a "taste town” promoting communal dining experiences.

Birch's world is challenged when the government mandates the exclusive usage of synthetically-produced ingredients in all daily meals to combat food scarcity brought on by global climate change. At the same time, Zephyr rallies against the advancements in AI-driven cooking technologies, arguing that cuisine is an extension of heritage tied intricately to human spirit—something that cannot be synthesized or replicated. Rad slowly brings a prototype to life, unlike anything seen before: the Recreate, a device that would enable anyone, including synthetic beings, to 'feel' the essence of cooking through experiences, community participation, and real-time recipes while guiding food-based AR interactions. 

Brokenhearted over the government’s localization policy and pressured by gastronomical artists forming opposition blocks in her supporters, Zephyr connects online with additional activists who are savvy in brewing mischief against the sterile glorification of the stovetop.
