Stovetops are no longer simple appliances but highly interactive culinary companions embedded with holographic projections. He is surrounded by a minor but loyal band: Lotus, a blend of vacationer and hacker hoping to dismantle corporate monopolies, Lucien, an elderly engineer, source of wondrous antiquities and wisdom, and Pippa, a curious AI who takes the form of a hovering orb, eager to experience “learning” through human connection. Together, they dream of recreating his signature dish, a transcendent eternal soup, once regarded as an alchemical masterpiece, using only the rumored rare resin harvested from a secret, Sustainable Cooking Collective.
