In the year 2042, the world had become dominated by futuristic conveniences, and one of the most revolutionary technologies was the Stovetop 3D, a kitchen device seamlessly integrating augmented reality (AR) and artificial intelligence (AI). Its capabilities extended far beyond simple cooking; it transformed everyday culinary tasks into immersive experiences. At the center of the tale is Mei, a passionate chef obsessed with maintaining the integrity of traditional recipes against the invasive perfectionism of AI, along with her sister Yu, a high-tech food engineer who views the advancement of cooking techniques as a boon for humanity. They are assisted by Jean, a PR mogul intent on branding the Stovetop 3D as the must-have culinary gadget, and Tai, an old-school restaurateur fighting against the modern tides to preserve age-old culinary artifacts and knowledge from the encroaching high-tech competitors.

As society races to award the new Stovetop 3D the "Gastronomic Creation Cup," a prestigious culinary competition, it becomes evident that outdated perceptions of cooking face grave challenges. Mei, determined to reclaim the rich flavors and deep stories contained in traditional dishes, enters the competition, championing authenticity. Meanwhile, Yu partners with Jean to optimize the Stovetop 3D's interface for embodied ease in the competition, showcasing its use for delivering unerring culinary beauties—calculating each ingredient’s reaction and measuring portion sizes to sublime precision. Conflict between Mei and Yu intensifies as traditionalist values clash with technologic ideals, stirring up deeper familial tensions.
