In the year 2045, cooking has evolved into an intricate blend of technology, artistry, and sustainability, and the primary cooking appliance of choice is the Stovetop Disk - a sleek, holographic array of magnetic coils and AI-driven sensors that adjust both temperature and cooking time based on biometric data from users. Melody, a brilliant home chef and lead designer at SmartCook, seeks to redesign the Stovetop Disk to incorporate ingredients that can – with just a flick of the wrist – combine and replicate gourmet meals based on users' memories. Meanwhile, Daryn, a cynical food critic disillusioned by the mechanisms of culinary excitement, aims to expose these artificial inventions that overshadow human cooking skill. Their lives intersect journalistic rivalry apart as both move through metropolitan kitchens teeming with aspiring gas-lit cooks and robotic sous chefs.

As Melody develops her prototype, she is challenged by key milestones: convincing investors of the device's worth while participating in a global culinary award known as “The Summit”. This event draws talent from across the globe, and petty rivalries become sharp as Daryn announces his presence to critique individual creations harshly. During intense working sessions, amidst collaborations, Melody meets Jasper, a dynamic influencer cheffing traditional meals but reliant on SmartCook technology to achieve their artistic visions without losing authenticity.
