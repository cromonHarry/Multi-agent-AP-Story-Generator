In the not-so-distant future, cooking has entered a transformative era driven by innovation and artificial intelligence. The Stovetop 2.0, a sleek, all-in-one culinary machine, sits prominently in domestic and professional kitchens alike. Governed by a powerful AI named GAI, it interfaces seamlessly with users via holographic projections, nearly eliminating the need for cookware altogether. With the ability to not only prepare food but to create customized meals that can account for individual dietary needs and preferences, lines between chef and machine have blurred. Our story follows four main characters: Isla, a passionate chef determined to preserve culinary arts; Dean, the corporate superstar behind the Stovetop 2.0 development; Aria, a nostalgic food blogger seeking to transmit through writing tastes from the past; and Baxter, a cynical food critic skeptical about technology stealing the essence of gourmet cooking.

Rapidly, the rise of the Stovetop 2.0 raises questions about the authenticity of food and emotion tied to cooking.
