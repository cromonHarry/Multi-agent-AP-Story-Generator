Dari, an ambitious culinary engineer, prides herself on her innovations with the Stovetop line. She collaborates closely with Mael, a robotics specialist slave android whose tinkerer's soul yearns for creativity, and Newt, her childhood friend, an app developer wanting to perfect an interface for these futuristic appliances.
