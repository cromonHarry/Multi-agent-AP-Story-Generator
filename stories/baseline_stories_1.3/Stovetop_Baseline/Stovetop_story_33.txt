In the bustling metropolis of Neo Alto in 2042, advanced home cooking tech has vastly transformed daily life; "Stovetop" is now the central hub of every kitchen, an AI-driven holographic cooking assistant that integrates with every appliance. Innovations like molecular gastronomy altered the way families shared meals, favoring convenience over slow-cooked wistfulness. Among those adapting to this new culinary landscape are Jasper, a once-esteemed food critic who has become disillusioned with robotic flavors; Meera, a gift-giving culinary architect whose designs amaze and dazzle; Roy, a software engineer desperate to combine food nostalgia with cutting-edge science, and Skye, an ambitious chef striving to bring authenticity back to fine dining amidst rewarmed food dished out by the Stovetop.

As demand for hyper-specialized cooking engines grows exponentially, a slick tech company launches "Culinary Nirvana," an upgrade for the Stovetop—promised to evolve every recipe into perfect palatability via neural learning. Fascinated yet skeptical, Jasper attends the launch event with Meera, hoping to be impressed, though he fears losing the artistry of cooking after witnessing Meera’s successful futuristic designs at work. Meanwhile, Roy falls down a deep rabbit hole of coding and causes a momentous glitch in the Culinary Nirvana. In rage-fueled confusion, he triggers an error that challenges not just the upgrading process but its influence on social foodscape, sparking these vividly orchestrated dishes with flavors extending deep into nostalgic realms—and jolts of neon sustenance.
