In the near future, the world thrives under a semi-ordered ascendance of technology aided by Artificial Intelligence in every aspect of life. The staple of daily cooking has transformed with the emergence of the "IntelliHearth," a smart stovetop that can simulate any flavor, from authentic home-cooked dishes to ultra-modern fusion cuisines. Amelia, a dedicated chef striving to impress the esteemed Gem Dining Society, harbors a vibrant culinary vision that seeks to revive traditional cooking artefacts. Basso, her AI assistant, programmed with knowledge from centuries of cooking literature, embodies recommendation algorithms and represents the unique usage of the IntelliHearth to its greatest potentials. Then there’s Roku, a strong culinary contrarian who despairs of technology’s interference with artistry, and Shiv, an innovative food-walking influencer eager to capitalize on both human culinary traditions and futuristic creations to stir social media buzz.
