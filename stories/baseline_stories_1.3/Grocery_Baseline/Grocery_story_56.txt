In 2045, humanity has fully embraced the convenience of meat and vegetable synthesis through rapid cellular regeneration technology, leading to towering grocery conglomerates that dominate the urban landscape. The vibrant markets of the past have transformed into subsectioned syndicates of efficiency, skimming fresh-from-the-lab produce into attractive glowing holographic displays. Emilia, a struggling pop-culture influencer with a dwindling following, is drawn to the flashiness of these stores. Her best friend Jax, an underground chef rebel opposing synthetic food, finds joy in his eccentric little eatery that specializes in grown biodiversity lost to GMO optimization and corporate control. Meanwhile, Reva, a tech genius and skilled programmer, leads risk-takers in the Grocery Anti-Monopoly Alliance (GAMA), eyeing to disrupt the suffocating food market's control. Dael is the oddly detached head engineer at Synergy Grocers, aware of the stirring discontent but too financially laden to risk position in the corporate hierarchy.

Over drinks in Jax's eatery, Emilia discovers Reva’s mission to hijack a shipment of highly enriched crops that can thrive even in the most polluted environments, possibly changing the power dynamic of biotechnology forever.
