In the year 2045, grocery shopping has transformed into an immersive experience through the Meld, a holographic virtual market interface that allows consumers to shop from the comfort of their homes. Tasha, a haptic designer who created a popular line of user gloves that enhanced the sensory experience, believes the Meld is the future, where shopping involves more than just exchanging currency for food; it offers an experience tailored for each individual. Marcus, her skeptical roommate, often reminisces about the “old days” of face-to-face shopping and the camaraderie found in community markets. Meanwhile, Amina, an AI-programmed grocery guide who learns customer preferences in real-time, knows enough to thread Tasha and Marcus’s different viewpoints into playful banter when they’re logged into the Meld together. Their friend Brian often participates too, specializing in creating healthy, personalized meal plans from the data provided by the AI system, thus further showcasing how intertwined technology has become with their daily culinary adventures in this polarized society.

As Maz re-enters the shopping arena, concerns surface in the community about the Stirred—a faction agitating for a return to traditional grocery shopping strategies to re-establish local cultures as the Meld allows aerosol frog bands and synthetic vegetables to flood the market. Simultaneously, Marcus convinces Brian that this new reality could lead to a disastrous oblivion of community ties if they don’t act soon.
