In the year 2045, society has undergone a revolution in how people procure food, thanks to the introduction of the Grocery Nexus, an advanced AI-enhanced portal that automates food delivery and strives for sustainability. This system, housed in towering, glass edifices known as G-Points, eliminates the traditional grocery store experience; no longer do consumers navigate aisles searching for items. Instead, within seconds of learning their dietary needs and real-time cravings through the Nexus app, individuals receive perfectly paired meals delivered within minutes—courtesy of autonomous drones. Our four protagonists each navigate this new dining frontier: Marie, an overworked single mom; Marcus, a driven tech entrepreneur; Clara, an environmental activist; and Jonas, a delivery drone technician who harbors secrets about the Nexus.
