In the year 2042, the concept of grocery shopping has drastically transformed. Enter Nova City, where intelligent drones ferry pre-packaged food capsules directly to consumers' homes. Traditional grocery stores have become relics of the past, merging shopping, cooking, and dining into a single, swift experience. Within this world pour multidimensional realities to navigate nutritional data and flavor profiles through wearable technologies. Our four protagonists, Timothy, a determined journalist; Nadia, an ingenious food scientist; Leo, a sharp-witted tech prodigy with an entrepreneurial spirit; and Cynthia, an elderly woman who longs for the simpler meals of days gone by, intertwine their lives in unpredictable ways as they confront the consequences of such capricious dietary technology.

As demand spikes for a specialized grocery service, the constellation of brands offering tailored foods inspired philosophy and psychology on nutrients reaches a boiling point. Timothy starts sniffing out a connection between the rapid decline of culinary skills and an unholy union between corporate ambitions and the health malaise plaguing society. Conspiracies bloom when he uncovers reports of covert practices in which corporations manipulate neurotriggers in food products to enthrall consumers. Meanwhile, Nadia becomes aware that her newest innovation, an molecular synthesis prototype which grows "prepared nutrients," may be unknowingly co-opted for these eldritch plans.
