In the year 2045, dental care has transcended beyond mere check-ups and fillings, evolving into a fusion of nanotechnology and virtual reality. PAS-995, an advanced AI-driven tooth-care vitual assistant, manages thousands of patients' health histories and carries out personalized preventative procedures derived from an individual's genomic makeup. Dr. Lila Sutherland, a dental surgeon who developed an affinity not only for dentistry but also for robotics and bioluminescence, leads the forefront of this transformation. Accompanying her is Max Jensen, an ambitious young dentist eager to learn, and Starr Ioan, a cutting-edge biotech programmer loyal to the revolution which dental care has embarked upon. Their rival, Dr. Mark Everett, offers outdated, traditional care focused solely on pain management and tangible treatments, often resorting to dangerously their past perfoced shortcuts.

As public distrust toward conventional dentists grows due to rising cases of Mercury syndromes - induced health issues blamed on mercury preservatives - Dr. Sutherland and her team find themselves competing against skepticism and traditions entrenched despite cutting-edge improvements. Launching a new initiative named "TranScendence," Dr. Lila seeks to demonstrate the seamless harmony between oral health investment and personal enhancements through minimalist surgeries conducted via light therapy harnessing sea coral DNA. This echoes the dual desire for beauty as well as safety amidst a population with disintegrating cliffs between outdated ideologies and technological grandiosity. Against this innovative backdrop, Max befriends an intellectually curious patient, Delia Carter, whose apprehensive outlook on modern dentistry opens new horizons for the team’s campaign.
