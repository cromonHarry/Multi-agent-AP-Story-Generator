Exudao Medical Corporation reigns as the leading bioengineer of dental innovations, previously run by the brilliant yet reclusive Dr. Lila Bhaskaran, whose cutting-edge techniques made dental visits less similar to archaic torture chambers and more like serene retreats. Amongst her team, Ethan Lawson is a former dental implants specialist struggling to adapt to a world where surgical skills have become obsolete; Mini Torres, an audacious programmer, has developed a revolutionary AI dentist — named DDS-3 — capable of conducting procedures with zero human interaction. They credit this service for the disappearing dental crises; yet therein lies the consumerism behind it, extracting remarkable pricing structures for personalized smiles in an otherwise broken healthcare system. As underground movements fighting against corporate deviances gain traction, Jax's sage experience ignites seminars foreshadowing doom, reminding a youth fascinated by algorithmic perfection of pockets where skilled mentality still thrives.
