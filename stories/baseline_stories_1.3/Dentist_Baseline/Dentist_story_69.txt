In the year 2042, dental practice has transcended its traditional boundaries, evolving into a high-tech blend of stem-cell biology, nanotechnology, and artificial intelligence. Automated clinics dot the skyline of megacities, attended by virtual reality dentists that alter the experience of dental care into something wondrous yet daunting. Amid the buzzing excitement of machinery and computation, Dr. June Harlow – a renowned human dentist facing obsolescence – grapples with professional insecurity. Her loyal assistant, Liz, a cheerful AI entity with a distinct personality, serves them during both routine check-ups and intricate procedures, often overshadowing the human element, while young Sam, June's apprentice, seeks validation in their skills as he adapts to this new world. The quirky patient Mr. Calver, a cyber-enhanced architect fascinated by technology, unwittingly becomes the linchpin striking at the tension between tradition and tomorrow.
