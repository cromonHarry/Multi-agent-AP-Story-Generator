In the year 2045, the profession of dentistry has transformed in remarkable ways, becoming deeply enmeshed with advanced technology and ethical dilemmas. In the bustling megalopolis of Neo-Sanctum, Dr. Elara Forst is known as one of the top holo-dentists, using a blend of virtual reality and AI to perform treatments painlessly. Patients don neural headgear, which immerses them in tranquil landscapes where their perceived experience of dental procedures is dramatically altered. The local authorities license Elara's innovative clinic, though rumors swirl around the black market where biohackers implant illegal mod chips to alter tooth decay metrics artificially. As people across the city place idealism and vanity above health, a movement emerges: the “True Dental Movement,” asserting that natural tooth health has been sacrificed by a reliance on chemical fixes and technology. 

This unsettling trend also draws in Jag, a tormented yet gifted hacker whose lifelong anxiety about dental hygiene manifests in a subtle obsession.
