In the year 2045, dental practice has transformed into a high-tech specialty that extends far beyond the traditional boundaries of oral health. In a world where dentistry has integrated seamlessly with cutting-edge biotechnology, patients can customize their teeth with vibrant microchips that not only improve aesthetics but also enhance functionality. Doctor Lena Marlowe, a forefront innovator and a vibrant smile architect, leads a team in her ultra-modern clinic located in NeoCity, where oral health intersects with virtual reality. She collaborates with her assistant Eva, a robotic hybrid who can analyze dental issues down to the genetic level, ensuring perfect solutions.
