In the year 2045, society is governed by an all-encompassing digital infrastructure. Virtually every aspect of life relies on an advanced neural web, seamlessly connecting people’s memories, thoughts, and responsibilities. Instead of passwords that can be forgotten or stolen, everyone now carries a neurolink and uses their unique mind patterns as their authentication process. However, illegal hacking syndicates have emerged, manipulating these reports to access personal information while leaving their targets without memory of the breach. Michiko, a skilled cybersecurity expert, is determined to safeguard the integrity of minds and tackle the growing wave of identity theft.

As Michiko investigates the recent uptick in memory thefts, she learns of a chilling underground organization dubbed "The Fracturers." Its leader, Drexon, a former neurolink developer, has weaponized users’ digital signatures against them.
