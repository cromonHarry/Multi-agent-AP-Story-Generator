In the year 2045, society had evolved into a hyper-connected digital landscape where everything from banking to personal relationships relied on biometrics and mind-linked devices. Humanity had transitioned away from traditional passwords to a seamless authentication process facilitated through NeuralLink chips embedded into every citizen’s brain. The system seamlessly merged thoughts and memories, requiring users to merely contemplate who they wanted to communicate with or what they needed to access. However, beneath this facade of safety lay a hidden world of data manipulation, cybercriminals who sought to exploit cognitive vulnerabilities, and a global initiative known as the HyperSecure Project intended to monitor and safeguard mental integrity. At the center of this revolution were four friends: data hacker Isha, cybernetic engineer Ravi, AI researcher Sarah, and digital anthropologist Theo.

Armed with their technological skills and a whimsical sense of curiosity, the group began dig into rumors surrounding an underground organization known as "The Anarchs." This group aimed to wrest control of NeuralLink, indicating that their success relied on feared hackers who could manipulate thought processes directly.
