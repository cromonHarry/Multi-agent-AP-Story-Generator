In the near future, the concept of "Bedtime" has radically transformed. After years of advancement in sleep technology, societies now rely on Sleep Optimization Units (S.O.U.s) that regulate the entire sleeping experience, allowing individuals to upload their successful sleep patterns into a shared database. Lena, a doctor specializing in sleep studies, finds herself at the forefront of this technological innovation alongside her partner, Assan, who works on the human-computer interface for S.O.U.s. Jenna, their tech-averse teenage daughter, rebels against the dependency on machines. Meanwhile, Arthor, an enigmatic former sleep scientist with a one-time connection to Lena, believes humans are sacrificing the sanctity of sleep for efficiency. 

As night falls in the sprawling city of Ossia, Lena distributes an upgrade to the S.O.U.s set to advance their neural syncing capabilities. Enthusiastic communities receive their upgrades, but Jenna protests vehemently, longing for the days when sleep was a ritual woven into the fabric of family life. Unbeknownst to most, Arthor secretly reprograms his own S.O.U. in hopes of igniting a crisis, sending insiders into a frenzy as wild electromagnetic pulsations disrupt nightly routines. Tensions rise when entire neighborhoods experience simultaneous sleep failures, masking pen-style nightmares with fears of bifurcated schedules, social rebellion, and alienation amid walls of algorithmic confinement.

In a fateful twist, Jenna decides to hack into her own family's S.O.U. to override the commands and add her lockdown patterned settings so that she can experience "real" sleep as she recalls from her childhood - stillness dotted with nuanced dreams. To her surprise, Arthor had foretold her direction. Merging with artificial waking illusions, chaos intensifies, and S.O.U.s go haywire, leading Assan to investigate the inner machine disruptions while Lena mourns their family's nostalgia for human connection unattached to technology. Jenna uncovers information hiding secrets about the very technology that promises to offer consorted solace, discovering its origins rooted in unethical practices that suffocate the very essence of familial warmth.

Even as complete disruption encapsulates the city, reality flows on the thin barriers separating comfortable power structures from subconscious malfunctions. Hurriedly, Lena races against time, confronting timeless questions about the essence of “Bedtime” to confront both Jenna’s rebellious actions and Arthor’s extremist measures. Suddenly, she envisions a blooming compromise, advocating for balancing technological advancements with hints of real-world experience – to accept implants without sacrificing their humanity.
