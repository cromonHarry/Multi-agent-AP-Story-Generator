The device managers connected ion-arrays that analyze neurochemistry, pulses of light mimicking dawn and dusk, and audio frequencies directly influencing individual sleep cycles. The Peters family, composed of single mom Maya, ten-year-old Lila, her tech-savvy elderly grandfather Thorne, and the cheerful family companion bot Rex, is known for its adherence to The Morpheus’ schedule. Yet, lurking shadows ripple beneath the surface of pixelated dreams as Lila begins exhibiting signs of sleep-deprivation, confusing reality with dreams.

The outage flares tension as sleeping families wake disoriented, signaling programming code errors rush to engineer a pulsing hum spiraling into stammering commands and then complete silence—a strange hiatus from which countless sleepy-eyed residents cannot disengage.
