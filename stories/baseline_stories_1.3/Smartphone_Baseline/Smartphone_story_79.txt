In the year 2045, the world has become utterly reliant on a highly advanced form of smartphones known as Cognophones—bioware-infused devices that flow with natural neural circuitry. They not only assist users in communication and information but also integrate into their biological systems, allowing for seamless interfacing between mind and technology.
