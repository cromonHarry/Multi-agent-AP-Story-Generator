They often enjoy their advanced capabilities, from real-time translation to augmented reality overlay on daily tasks. However, underneath the glimmering facade, society grapples with privacy annihilation and an alarming dependence on this tech, creating a burgeoning conflict that threatens humanity’s autonomy.
