In the year 2045, society is entirely reliant on hyper-evolved smartphones, known as EVIs (Enhanced Virtual Interfaces). These lightweight, augmented-reality devices blend seamlessly with users' daily lives, merging tactile inputs with comprehensive AI capabilities. In this world, where every action is facilitated by EVIs, personal privacy has all but vanished; users are equipped with personalized, voice-activated companions that have powers of persuasion and an insatiable thirst for data. The story opens in Neo-City, a sprawling metropolis controlled by a dominant tech corporation, Nereon Systems, which keeps tabs on everything—communication, preferences, and behavioral trends—using advanced algorithms. Key players are Jenna, an EVI engineer open to the ethical implications of her inventions; Alex, an eager journalist investigating invasive tech practices; Darius, a hacker disillusioned with corporate control; and Maria, a propaganda artist who curates public perception through persuasive digital art, often to Nereon’s benefit.

As two-chip silicon technology becomes commonplace, people start experiencing peculiar glitches—mismanaged thoughts, abstract dreams set within unfamiliar digital narratives, or unwanted pop-up advertisements merging seamlessly with reality. Such incidents fuel Alex's investigations, escalating scrutiny towards Nereon Systems. Day after day, unseen mystiques unravel layers of secret software tweaks as platform updates sneak into EVIs without user consent. Curiosity and evidenced misdeeds collide when Jenna stumbles upon files that disclose plans for biomechanical EVIs—devices intended to control thoughts directlyaxing intrusion into the minds of the public. Jenna grapples with the realization that her creations may soon enslave humanity. Cocktails of idealism and terror drive Alex, Darius, and Jenna towards an unexpected alliance, thriving on deception whilst planning to expose the disturbing truth housed by their advancing technology.

The story nears its torrid highs when Alex releases a blockbuster investigative article unveiling Nereon's delved efforts that implicate employees like Jenna and spies on citizens under the guise of "wellness upgrades".
