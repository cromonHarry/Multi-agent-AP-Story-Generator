Her intrigue deepens when she encounters two young engineers working on polarizing projects designed to either deepen or disrupt the Symbiont’s connection with users: Theo, who is convinced that the device can lead to unprecedented human achievement, and Mara, a whistleblower who believes the Symbiont endangers free will and society’s autonomy.
