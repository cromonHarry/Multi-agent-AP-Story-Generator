In the year 2147, dental care has become emblematic of societal stratification. Outside the pristine, gated clinics where the elite receive their advanced AI-driven treatments, Lana Zade rallies the marginalized. As the passionate leader of the 'Smile Rebels', she utilizes artistic expression, like bioluminescent murals, to raise awareness about the 'Dental Lives Matter' movement, which challenges the profound inequities fueled by a digital dental caste system. Dr. Suri Akash, a visionary technologist, secretly devises affordable dental technologies to support this grassroots movement, while Dorian Kappa, a wealthy dental tycoon, grapples with his conflicting desire for equitable care amidst pressures to maintain his elite clientele. In the shadows, Tyler Voss crafts at-home solutions that defy the monopolized access to health, weaving together a tapestry of rebellion against the commodification of oral well-being.
