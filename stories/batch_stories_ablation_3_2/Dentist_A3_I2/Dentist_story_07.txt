In the vibrant city of Greenhaven, the sun casts a warm glow over the lush canopies that interweave with the biotanical clinics known as 'Living Eco-Capsules.' Among them, Finn O'Connor, a passionate community activist, uses augmented reality to unveil the often overlooked disparities in dental care. Meanwhile, Nia Kurobushi meticulously designs plant-based dental treatments in her lab, integrating AI bio-fabrication to benefit the local ecosystem. Zeke Rivermore, an ecologist, leads discussions on how community and ecology coexist, igniting a collective vision of a healthier planet. Together, they kindle hope in a world where health and the environment thrive in harmony, setting the stage for an unfolding revolution in Eco-Dentistry.
