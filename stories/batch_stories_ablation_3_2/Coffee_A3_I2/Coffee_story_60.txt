The night crescendos in a heated debate on the role of coffee as social currency, forging a united front against the looming corporate shadow, transforming the lounge into a sanctuary of resilience and community activism.

As augmented reality immerses attendees in powerful recollections of resilience and resistance, the corporate powerhouse crashes the event, threatening to erase their narrative.
