Orin Vale, a visionary Bio-Adaptive Space Engineer, tinkers with spaces that breathe with their users' sentiments, lost in his quest to find authenticity amid the fabric of his engineered environments. Together, they inhabit a world where trust and identity are fluid, yet each grapples with their place in a society defined by the emotions they share and create.
