In the year 2045, the field of dentistry has undergone a radical transformation, shaped by advances in technology and a growing emphasis on wellness over mere hygiene. In this world, dentists are no longer simply caretakers of teeth but rather bioengineers who specialize in the comprehensive health of the oral biome. Vira, a talented young bioscientist, works at a cutting-edge facility known as Oralis, where she uses nanotechnology to not only treat dental problems but to enhance overall health through oral therapies. Her mentor, Dr. Lyle Kent, a seasoned dental innovator, is revered for his groundbreaking research on oral microbiomes and their link to systemic wellness. Together, they aim to develop a new treatment protocol that could prevent diseases far beyond dental issues. Their ambitious project faces skepticism from the conservative dental community, which still clings to traditional methods.

As Vira and Dr. Kent finalize their product, they encounter Emory, a charismatic but reckless patient. Emory, an avid vlogger and influencer, sees undergoing the new treatment as a chance to boost her online persona. Initially, she seems like the perfect candidate to showcase their breakthrough, but her lack of discipline poses significant challenges.
