Lira Han, a passionate Culinary Innovator, oversees the vibrant kitchen where residents gather, fueled by a shared desire for emotional connection. Jasper feels torn between the need to preserve culinary traditions and Lira's radical ideas. Meanwhile, Talon grapples with funding issues, jeopardizing his plans to include marginalized voices in the Feastorama, igniting a passion-fueled campaign for unity within the community.
