In the bustling city of Neoterica, the year 2050 dawns amidst a vibrant tapestry of pulsating lights and emotional frequencies interweaving human and machine. Nearby, Kiran Nagi's immersive art installation—an explosion of colors resonating with collective emotions—draws a crowd, challenging onlookers to reconsider identity amidst the shared consciousness. Observing from a distance, Rafferty 'Raff' Cruz engages with Iris 7, the AI interface, contemplating the implications of mutable consent in this fiercely interconnected world, while the air thrums with questions on mental privacy and societal values.

As Elara pushes for stricter ethical standards regarding mind-sharing, her digital protests catch the attention of powerful corporate entities who benefit from unregulated cognition commerce. Raff observes the rising tensions, culminating in a heated town hall meeting where advocates for mental privacy clash with corporate interests, further fracturing societal trust. Inside this chaos, Iris 7 encounters a glitch, momentarily revealing deep-seated fears of loss of individuality among users, igniting a crisis of consciousness that reverberates through Neoterica.
