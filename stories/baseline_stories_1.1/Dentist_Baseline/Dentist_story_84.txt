In the year 2045, dental care has transformed into a high-tech endeavor with the rise of advanced AI systems and smart nanobots that can repair and enhance teeth at the molecular level. In this era, traditional dentists have largely been replaced by "Neuromolecular Denserves," who specialize in the conscious modulation of oral health through programmed nano-services. The protagonist, Juno Tael, is a passionate young Denserse working at a state-of-the-art facility, Orbit Dental. She thrives on blending technology with the compassionate touch once associated with the old profession. Her best friend, Kai, is an AI ethicist who constantly debates the moral implications of robotic treatments, arguing that patient care should not lose its human essence. Their boss, Dr. Rhea Maris, a trailblazer in dental technology, drives innovation but becomes increasingly dehumanized as she advocates for efficiency over empathy.

As Juno deftly navigates the world of nano-repair and virtual consultations, she stumbles upon a troubling pattern: patients seem to be suffering side effects from unintentional crosslinking in their nano-treatments, disrupting not only dental health but potentially their neural connections. In her hunt for truth, Juno recruits Kai and her tech-savvy brother, Leo, to assist her in sifting through archived data files and treatment protocols. With multiple reports from various practitioners indicating similar complications, it quickly becomes clear that the new treatments offered by the facility have led to unanticipated consequences. Meanwhile, Juno must confront the ambitious Dr. Maris, who insists that any evidence of malfunction could shutter the facility and tarnish the state-of-the-art reputation they've built.

The pressure mounts as Juno analyzes the data she'd collected, almost certain that Dr. Maris knew of these risks but suppressed evidence for the sake of progress. During a clinical conference at Orbit Dental, she confronts Dr. Maris publicly. Creating a rift within the community, Juno calls for a moratorium on all nanobot treatments while urging a thorough investigation.
