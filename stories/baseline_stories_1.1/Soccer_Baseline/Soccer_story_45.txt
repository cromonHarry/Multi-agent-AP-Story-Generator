In the year 2042, the world of sports has been transformed by technology, science, and humanity's enduring love for competition. Soccer, once a simple game of footwork and cleverness, has evolved into a high-octane spectacle known as "Hyper Soccer." Played in stadiums designed as zero-gravity arenas, games are enhanced by augmented reality and player-administered exoskeletons for optimal performance. The fans are more engaged than ever, sporting brain-computer interfaces that allow them to feel the adrenaline rush and pain of their favorite players. Among the standout stars of this world is Zara, a fiercely precise striker with the heart of a lion, whose secret weapon is a neural training app that allows her to anticipate opponents' moves. Alongside her is Marcus, a tactical genius and captain of Team Veloce, who struggles with the moral implications of using performance-enhancing tech. Competing against them is Jude, an AI-driven athlete feared for his flawless calculations and predictability. The fourth main character is Elise, a sports journalist aiming to unveil the truth behind the technology that is altering the essence of the game.
