Jalen Keats passionately discusses his latest Edumorphic project, a vibrant tapestry of nightmares, with Tamsin Vora, who advocates fervently for individual rights within dreamscapes. Together, they navigate a society where creativity meets responsibility, poised on the brink of an intellectual revolution fostered by dreams.
