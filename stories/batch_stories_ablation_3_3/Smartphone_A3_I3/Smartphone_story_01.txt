Meanwhile, Theo Varun rallies crowds, wielding his passion for mental sovereignty as he leads dissent against cognitive entrapment, urging individuals to reclaim their lost identities. Myra Solis curates evocative thought-sharing festivals, her art weaving the threads of communal experience while whispering the need for personal expression. Overseeing this delicate balance is Dorian Huxley, tasked with enforcing a global 'Cognitive Code' that clashes with the rising call for individuality, setting the stage for a societal conflict that could reshape the very essence of self.
