Nearby, Reef Kader prepares the ground for the upcoming Flavorformance Festival, envisioning it as a celebration of shared human narratives through taste. As Juno Maris monitors the emotional data streams that flicker across her digital interface, she senses the palpable energy of the community. Meanwhile, Kai Fernandes applies the final brush strokes to his latest BrewJackers artwork—a colorful mural highlighting the intimacy of coffee-drinking culture.
