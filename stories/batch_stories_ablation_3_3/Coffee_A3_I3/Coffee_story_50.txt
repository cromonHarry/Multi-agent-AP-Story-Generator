Meanwhile, Kai Solis shares stories around communal tables, fostering bonds that transcend technology, while Rhea Draven advocates for ethical sourcing through culinary festivals. As they navigate an evolving cultural landscape, the undercurrents of digital coffee identity theft loom, threatening the authenticity of their cherished rituals.
