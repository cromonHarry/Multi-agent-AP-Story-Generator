Dylan Rodriguez, an AI developer, tirelessly refines the algorithms powering intelligent nanobots that guide shoppers through their choices, transforming consumers into conscious stewards of the environment. Meanwhile, Evelyn Moss tirelessly works to bridge the gap between local farmers and consumers, advocating for food equity through active engagement and participatory governance. In this dynamic landscape, evenFinn Reeve, a holographic chef, reimagines cooking through augmented reality, inviting locals back to their communal kitchens in an effort to restore the lost art of home-cooked meals.
