In the year 2045, the concept of bedtime has transformed into a nightly ritual dominated by technology. Children no longer sleep in traditional beds; instead, they nestle into personal Sleep Pods, equipped with neuro-sync technology that directly interfaces with their subconscious. This advancement helps children learn while they dream, allowing them to absorb knowledge and skills at an astonishing rate. As they navigate this brave new world, they each experience the benefits and pitfalls of their nightly rituals.
