In the year 2045, the world has transitioned into a hyper-connected society where cooking has become a communal digital experience. Stovetops, once mere tools for heating and preparing food, are now multi-sensory experiences embedded with artificial intelligence. The new "StoveNet" system connects individual stovetops worldwide, allowing people to share recipes, flavors, and even emotional resonance linked to dishes. Tessa, a recent food-tech engineer, works for Adaptive Cuisine, helping to launch the next-generation stove, dubbed the "SoulChef." Meanwhile, her best friend Kai, an aspiring culinary artist, struggles to gain recognition in an industry dominated by fine dining and digital illusions. Ruth, Tessa's older mentor, is skeptical about losing traditional values in cooking, while Leo, a world-renowned chef, champions the SoulChef as the future of gastronomy.
