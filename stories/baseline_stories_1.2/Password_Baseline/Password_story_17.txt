In the year 2045, identity verification comes with indefinitely advancing technology, where passwords have evolved into personalized BioSigns: a unique fingerprint of one’s biological and digital identity. Urban landscapes are dotted with glowing interfaces that read retinal patterns, heartbeat rhythms, and even neural signatures. VEEA, a high-tech security analyst, finds herself embroiled in a sinister cyber-network known as the Rook, where stolen BioSigns are traded for access to exclusive possessions and sensitive information. VEEA’s former colleague, IMAN, a hacker-turned-informant, tiptoes the line between loyalty and self-preservation, while AGEL, an elite employee of the tech conglomerate BioSecurity, tirelessly strives to uncover the truth behind this undeniable connection between criminals and the plethora of stolen identities.

As threats from the Rook escalate, VEEA’s closest ally, LIAM, a bold investigator well-versed in the underbelly of the cyber world, gets a tip about an impending heist that could result in the theft of thousands of BioSigns. A haunting image of several innocent victims affected in the past hangs over him, and time is of the essence. As the unlikely team—VEEA, IMAN, AGEL, and LIAM—gathers in the neon-lit heart of the city, their distinct skills emerge, inching closer to unraveling the operation behind the heist. Revenue streams for Rook are confirmed to come from dissecting these BioSigns to generate illicit social power based on the stolen identities. The tension frays as dissent grows between IMAN and AGEL, who both have deeply-rooted reasons behind their roles within the circuit, making them confrontational over the validity of exposing the network.

With a locational scan putting the operation in a nondescript building ideally situated in the techno-industrial sector, the team gears up to confront the Rook members. Detailing their plan, emotions run high as sharp discord stands in their path. At the height of the confrontation—where betrayal runs amok—IMAN’s past catches up to him, surrounding him with memories of his myriad reasons for switching allegiances. Agitated, he compromises their position to solicit his hidden room inside the Rook’s design. Confronting the leader of the network, a shadow-cloaked figure known only as MONARCH, leads to frantic negotiations regarding the fate of unmodified human consciousness, as secrets fold into dark realities where consciousness is nanoscaled and commoditized into this new equilibrating future.

Just as they grasp the essence of the contested network through stowed screens and data chains, LIAM initiates an ambush, intending to shutter Rook’s control over the dissemination of stolen data for revolutionizing identities. In attempt to rescue his friends amidst this chaos, the conflict presses, prominent emotions of fear and pull revolve the hall not only around their lives but all of collective humanity.
