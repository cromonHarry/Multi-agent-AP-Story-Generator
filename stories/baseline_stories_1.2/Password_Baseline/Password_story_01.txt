Gone were the days of typing forgotten passwords into clunky devices; in this near future, a neural-link synchronization—a direct connection between human consciousness and the digital cloud—enabled users to access their identities, finances, and personal data with thought alone. But amidst this supposed security, a shadow of fear loomed. She found herself reluctantly partnered with Caleb, the hot-shot tech entrepreneur whose ambitious vision of a unified digital humanity had splintered after the latest mass identity theft, Maria, a software vigilante driven by justice, and Ryker, an undercover agent from the Department of Digital Integrity aiming to shut down the unlawful virtual underground.

Despite their differences, motives intertwined, driving the four deeper into a covert world where friends might turn foes in an instant, and trust was worth its weight in gold—a currency falling apart at the seams.
