Desmond, an investigator tasked with unraveling the hack, is determined to reveal the identity of Evelyn, who can access and manipulate any system. Lastly, Layla, a spirited journalist searching for the truth, seeks to expose the corporate overlord's abuses by collaborating with Evelyn for an explosive news story.

As tensions rise in the city divided by technology and oppression, Evelyn connects with Layla to exchange information and develop a plan to expose the government's subversive activities. Devising a scheme, they intend to broadcast their findings using a rogue satellite network. However, unbeknownst to them, Harrison and Desmond are entangled in a chase after discovering traces of the hacking intrusion. Harrison is torn between duty and the fragmented trust that still lingers from his relationship with Evelyn.
