In the year 2045, coffee has become a rare and prized substance, largely due to climate collapse and agricultural upheaval. Synthesized alternatives have flooded the market, but none capture the authentic taste and aroma of real coffee. People rely on virtual reality experiences within vibrant coffee shops to feel a sense of nostalgia for genuine brews. Amelia, a barista skilled in the art of producing espresso from ancient, meticulously cultivated beans, works in a retro cafe in New Seattle that has resisted the trend. She finds joy in her work alongside Javier, her childhood friend and environmentally conscious advocate who fights for biodiversity initiatives. Meanwhile, Dr. Mira Novak, a biochemist working for the coffee conglomerate MegaBean Corp, believes she is on the verge of rediscovering the process to genetically engineer coffee plants resilient enough to withstand climate change. Unbeknownst to them, Vega, a corporate espionage agent assigned by MegaBean Corp, has infiltrated their community, intent on sabotaging their efforts to siphon their innovative practices and reclaim a monopoly on the market.
