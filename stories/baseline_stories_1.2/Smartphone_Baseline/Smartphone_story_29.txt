In the year 2045, smartphones have transcended their original form as handheld devices and evolved into sleek augmented reality (AR) implants known as iNeurons. These neural extensions connect directly with the user’s brain, allowing for instant access to information, telepathic communication, and immersive virtual experiences. The world is governed by technocratic organizations like Synapse Corp, which monopolizes dreams, thoughts, and digital realities. Amid this society, Alice, a mid-level data analyst haunted by the oppressive nature of constant connectivity, yearns for the simplicity of tangible existence. In contrast, Ethan, a charismatic cyber-activist and Alice’s older brother, embraces the enhancements proudly, advocating for their capabilities in fighting inequality. Romero, an elderly engineer, provides the necessary technical expertise to keep the resistance’s guerilla tactics functional. Lastly, Maris, an enigmatic hacker inheriting the legacy of lost digital freedom, represents an evolution of the underground movements built to reclaim autonomy from the all-seeing corporate eye.

As discontent grows within society’s fringes, Alice is drawn into Ethan's mission to expose the devious operation of Synapse Corp, responsible for developing a terrifying feature that can control emotions and thoughts through iNeuron implants. With Romero’s guidance, Ethan gathers a small team of fed-up citizens, promising them liberation through digital sabotage. Meanwhile, Maris infiltrates corporate systems, providing the technical prowess necessary to breach lodes of strategic secrets. They form a fragile alliance driven by an impulsive desire to restore individualism. As each member grapples with their reasons for resistance against over-dependence on their once-simple smartphones, internal conflicts entwine them into the purpose; fear merges with camaraderie, growing stronger against their shared enemy.

When the use of thought-manipulating technology is revealed to be imminent, the group devises a high-stakes plan: they seek to hack into the servers of Synapse Corp to disable the emotion-controlling program before it goes live during a global summit.
