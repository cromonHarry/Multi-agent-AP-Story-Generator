In the year 2143, the world is a mosaic of Bio-Tech Tribes and Shadow Haven Recluses, shaped by neuro-synaptic identity tokens that redefine privacy. Meanwhile, Ezra Voss, an innovative tech visionary, develops inclusive biometric systems, believing they can foster communal trust without erasing individuality. As Rhea Asher challenges the tension between intimacy and surveillance through her provocative art, Kieran Holt, a Bio-Trust Governance Inspector, reflects on his enforcement role, torn between collective security and preserving personal identity. Together, they navigate the choppy waters of a society grappling with trust and autonomy.
