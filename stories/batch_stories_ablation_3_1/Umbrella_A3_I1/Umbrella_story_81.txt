As an environmental journalist, she feels a gnawing sense of unease beneath the surface of this artificial paradise. Meanwhile, Sage Novo, the ambitious leader of the Eco-Theocracy, pushes narratives that mask the slow decay of the real world beyond their illusion, positioning himself as a guardian of this manufactured order. Unbeknownst to them, their interconnected destinies are poised to unravel the threads of the eco-illusion that binds them all.

While Sage tightens his grip over the media, wary of these mounting threats, the tension escalates, forcing the four into a collision path where their beliefs on identity, truth, and resistance will determine their fates and that of their world.
