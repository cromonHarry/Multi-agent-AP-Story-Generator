Meanwhile, Zyra Ingram observes from a nearby workstation, fine-tuning her AI programming to ensure the stove captures not just flavors but the essence of connection. Just as the festival teeters on the brink of failure, Aria’s intuitive embrace of shared experience ignites hope, transforming anxiety into a vibrant tapestry of diverse flavors that celebrates healing through connection.
