She meticulously engineers these cultivars to enhance their ecosystems, aiming to share their vibrant stories through unique flavors. Nearby, Felix Hartman prepares for his shift at the café, where he plans to weave the tales of local biodiversity into the coffee-drinking experience, using the narrative blends Amara created. Their collective ingenuity begins to shape a grassroots response to the changing environment, weaving a narrative of hope and innovation into their daily ritual.
