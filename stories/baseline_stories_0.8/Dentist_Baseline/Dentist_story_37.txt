In the year 2045, dental care has become a blend of advanced technology and artificial intelligence, with routines that involve the use of nanobots and virtual reality. The profession of "dentist" has transformed into a high-tech practice where patients can have their dental issues diagnosed and treated from the comfort of their homes.
