It operates via thought, eliminating the need for physical devices. People like Leo, a tech-savvy engineer, and Mira, a talented virtual artist, have embraced this technology, using it to enhance their daily lives. Meanwhile, Max, a brilliant but reclusive hacker, exploits the vulnerabilities of the system, seeking to expose its hidden dangers while remaining in the shadows.

As society becomes increasingly dependent on the NeuroLink, Leo and Mira thrive in their respective fields, designing intricate virtual worlds and developing neural applications that revolutionize entertainment.
