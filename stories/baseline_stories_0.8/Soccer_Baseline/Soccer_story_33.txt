As the opening match approaches, tensions escalate both on and off the field, setting the stage for a confrontation that will test the limits of their loyalty and resolve.

With time running out, Zara must decide whether to continue with the current strategy or pivot to save Lila and the integrity of the game. The tension peaks as they find themselves down by two goals with just minutes left on the clock, forcing the team to confront their deeper motivations for playing Hyperball.

In the aftermath of the intense match, the team reflects on their experiences, realizing that their individual desires and societal pressures have nearly destroyed their bond. Lila takes the lead, using her natural instincts to score a goal that unites the team and the crowd, embodying the true spirit of soccer. Although they do not win the championship, their performance earns respect and admiration, focusing attention on the need for change in the sport and society at large.
