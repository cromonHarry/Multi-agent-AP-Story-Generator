In the year 2045, society has embraced a revolutionary concept known as "SleepSync," a neural technology that allows individuals to sync their sleep patterns with one another, eliminating the need for traditional bedtime rituals. This age of hyper-connectivity has transformed the way people rest, with personalized sleep pods that utilize AI to optimize every aspect of slumber. The central characters include Maya, a sleep technician who pioneers SleepSync technology; Leo, an insomniac and a skeptic of the new systems; Zane, a child prodigy who has never experienced natural sleep; and Elara, a journalist investigating the societal impacts of SleepSync. As children like Zane grow up without the ritual of bedtime stories or dreams, Elara fears the loss of imagination and emotional connection within families.
